Campus Life



Since the last two decades "GitaRam", the name has been synonymous with trust. Trust that has been won and maintained by the GitaRam people, who have made it their mission to give back to the society more than what it has drawn from it for sustenance. It gives me immense pleasure to tell you that in the year 1999 GitaRam Education and Health Foundation had decided to come forward and play a leading role in transforming our beloved motherland as an ideal state in terms of peace, social, security, moral and ethical values through the highest magnitude of dedication, devotion, commitment and whole lot of sacrifice. Since 1999, we are offering its contribution in the field of Education and Health service to the society. GitaRam Hospital is a prospering regional autonomous hospital that serves the entire Murshidabad including the neighbouring districts by providing advance healthcare services that is not only affordable to privileged class but also underprivileged communities. In education service, we believe in continuous upgradation of infrastructural facilities along with quality development programs for faculty and enhancement of the work environment in all respects. Our teaching standards are consistently rated among the best. Qualified and dedicated faculties are our strength. Our high educational standards are reflected in the excellent achievements of our Alumni having well received in organisations, reputed worldwide. Last but not the least, I wish to express my gratitude to all those parents who put their valuable trust with us by giving opportunity to contribute for the career and moral development of their children. Let us take the pledge that “We will arm every student in GitaRam with the powerful WEAPON OF EDUCATION, to acquire strength of wisdom for the good of the world”
